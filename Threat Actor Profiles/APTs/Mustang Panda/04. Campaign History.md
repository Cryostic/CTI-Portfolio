# 04. Campaign History

<h2>Campaign Timeline</h2>

<h3>Establishment Phase (2012-2017)</h3>
<p>Defining Characteristics: PlugX-centric; APAC NGO and Minority Group focus; below public radar</p>

```
2012
└── Mustang Panda assessed to begin operations
    └── PlugX (first documented 2008) adopted as primary implant
    └── Focus: APAC governments, Mongolian/Tibetan targets

2015
└── Bookworm Trojan documented (Unit 42 / Palo Alto)
    └── Modular architecture foreshadowing later ToneShell design

2016 (May onward)
└── First documented targeting of U.S. entities begins
    └── Campaigns targeting U.S.-based think tanks and policy organizations
```

<h3>Public Emergence & NGO Targeting (2017-2019)</h3>
<p>Defining Characteristics: First public disclosure; Mongolian/Vietnamese themed lures; PlugX + Poison Ivy</p>

```
2017 (April)
└── CrowdStrike Falcon Intelligence observes previously unattributed 
    actor with Chinese nexus targeting U.S. based think tank
    └── Unique TTPs distinguish from known actors
    └── Mongolian-language lures suggest focus on Mongolia intelligence

2018 (June)
└── CrowdStrike formally names group "MUSTANG PANDA"
    └── "Meet CrowdStrike's Adversary of the Month" : first public profile

2018–2019
├── Anomali identifies 15 malicious documents; activity Nov 2018–Aug 2019
├── Primary target: Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV) ruling party
├── Secondary targets: UN Security Council lures; German NGOs; Myanmar; Pakistan
└── Tools: PlugX, Poison Ivy, custom loaders

2019 (Ongoing Myanmar Campaign Begins)
├── Myanmar government websites breached; malware hosted on official [.]gov[.]mm domains
├── Meterpreter deployed via malicious archives on Myanmar government website
├── Cisco Talos: "The threat actor has aggressively targeted the government of 
│   Myanmar since 2019, even breaching their websites on multiple occasions"
└── This becomes the group's most sustained single-country campaign
```

<h3>Geopolitical Surge & Full Custom Toolkit (2022–2023)</h3>
<p>Defining Characteristics: Ukraine-crisis exploitation; Earth Preta global campaign; ToneShell maturation; VSCode abuse</p>

```

├── Russian invasion triggers immediate Mustang Panda campaign expansion
├── Cisco Talos and Proofpoint document European diplomatic targeting within weeks
├── Lures: EU policy documents on Ukraine; Ukrainian NSDC official statements
├── Targets: EU member state foreign ministries; NATO-aligned diplomatic entities
├── New tool: TCP reverse shell via cmd.exe (parallel to PlugX chains)
└── Analysis: Beijing urgently needed to understand EU foreign policy realignment

2022 (May–November) : Earth Preta Global Campaign (Trend Micro)
├── Targets: Myanmar, Australia, Philippines, Japan, Taiwan (primary)
├── Method: Fake Google accounts distributing malware via Google Drive links
├── Lures: Geopolitically themed documents; archives (RAR/ZIP/JAR)
├── New tools: TONEINS installer, TONESHELL v1 backdoor, PUBLOAD downloader
└── AIDE/GCA telemetry confirms surge; peak activity October–November 2022

2022 (July)

2022 (November)
└── Trend Micro: ToneShell first publicly documented
    └── Custom backdoor suite accelerating to replace PlugX as primary tool

2023 (February)

    └── EU regulatory lures targeting European organizations

2023 (August)

2023 (September)

2024 (September)
```
