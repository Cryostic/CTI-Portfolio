# 01. Executive Summary

<p>
  OceanLotus or APT32 is one of the most technically capable and operationally disciplined Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) groups operating in the Asia-Pacific and Southeast Asia region. 
  Active since at least 2012, the group conducts long-term intrusion campaigns targeting foreign governments, multinational corporations with Vietnamese business interests, 
  civil society organizations, journalists, human rights defenders, and Vietnamese diaspora communities worldwide. The group maintains a diverse and actively developed malware ecosystem spanning Windows, 
  macOS, Linux, and Android platforms.
</p>

<p>
  APT32's operations directly serve Vietnamese state intelligence collection requirements, economic espionage, geopolitical surveillance, suppression of political dissidents, and competitive intelligence gathering. 
  Organizations with operations in or relationships with Vietnam across any of the following sectors face elevated and ongoing risk: government & diplomacy, manufacturing, automotive, technology, NGOs, and media. 
  The group's proven capability to develop cross-platform malware, abuse trusted services (GitHub, Google Docs) for C2, and conduct multi-year intrusions without detection underscores their sophistication.
</p>
