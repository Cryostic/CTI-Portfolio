# 04. Campaign History

<h2>Campaign Timeline</h2>

```
2012  ─── Initial activity identified; attacks on Chinese entities
          └── EFF staff receive phishing emails with OceanLotus malware
          
2014  ─── FireEye formally designates group APT32
          └── Targeting of foreign corporations with Vietnam business interests begins
          
2016  ─── Cobalt Kitty Campaign (Cybereason)
          └── Year-long intrusion against unnamed Asia-based multinational
          └── IP, confidential business data, and project details exfiltrated
          
2017  ─── OceanLotus Blossoms Campaign (FireEye/Mandiant)
          └── Mass surveillance targeting ASEAN nations, media, human rights groups
          └── Formal FireEye publication: "APT32 and the Threat to Global Corporations"
          └── Targeting of manufacturing, hospitality, and consumer goods sectors
          
2018  ─── Automotive Espionage Campaign begins
          └── BMW, Toyota, Hyundai targeted for trade secrets
          └── Timing correlates with Vietnam's launch of domestic automaker VinFast
          
2019  ─── BMW and Hyundai Breach Confirmed (Bayerischer Rundfunk report)
          └── BMW detected intrusion but monitored attackers for months before eviction
          └── German VDA issues warning to automotive industry members
          
2020  ─── COVID-19 Intelligence Campaign
          └── Targeting: Chinese Ministry of Emergency Management
          └── Targeting: Wuhan City Government
          └── Targeting: ASEAN health ministries
          └── Goal: Pandemic response intelligence, outbreak data
          
2021  ─── Civil Society Campaign (Amnesty International)
          └── Vietnamese bloggers, pro-democracy figures targeted
          └── Bui Thanh Hieu (Vietnamese dissident blogger) targeted
          └── VOICE (Vietnamese Overseas Initiative for Conscience Empowerment) targeted
          
2022–2023 ─── Continued SEA targeting; macOS malware updates
               └── RotaJakiro (Linux variant) documented
               └── PhantomLance Android campaign (Google Play)
               
2024 (Aug) ─── Multi-year NGO Intrusion Disclosed
               └── Vietnamese human-rights organization breached over multiple years
               └── Custom backdoors + scheduled-task/registry persistence
               └── Selective data exfiltration confirmed
               
2024 (Sep–Oct) ─── GitHub Supply Chain Campaign (ThreatBook)
                   └── Target: Chinese cybersecurity researchers and enterprises
                   └── Method: Poisoned GitHub repositories (Visual Studio projects)
                   └── Malware: Trojan.CobaltGate (backdoored Cobalt Strike plugin)
                   └── Infrastructure: GitHub Pages domains for exfiltration
                   
2025 ─── Developer Ecosystem Targeting Escalation
         └── Backdoored Cobalt Strike plugin distributed on GitHub
         └── Target: Security professionals globally
         └── Method: Weaponizing trusted developer workflows
         └── Demonstrates shift toward supply-chain/dev-ecosystem abuse
```
