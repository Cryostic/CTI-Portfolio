# 01. Executive Summary

<p>
  Active since at least 2009, Patchwork - also known as Dropping Elephant, Quilted Tiger, Operation Hangover, Chinastrats, MONSOON, Zinc Emerson, and APT-C-09 - is an Indian-nexus cyber espionage group publicly disclosed in 2016 and confirmed actively operating through 2025. 
  The group operates with probable state backing from Indian intelligence services, conducting espionage campaigns primarily targeting Pakistan, China, and entities associated with Indian strategic competitors.

  Named "Patchwork" by Cymmetria researchers who noted the group's code was assembled from publicly available online sources, literally "copy-pasted" from forums and GitHub. The group is analytically distinctive for 
  demonstrating that **sophisticated targeting does not require sophisticated code**. Over a decade of documented operations, Patchwork has successfully penetrated Pakistani military and government organizations, 
  Chinese diplomatic and nuclear research institutions, and a range of NGOs and think tanks across South and Southeast Asia using tools that were, at their foundation, cobbled together from the public internet.
</p>

<p>
  Organizations in Pakistan's defense, government, diplomatic, and telecommunications sectors face the highest threat level in this group's history. Chinese diplomatic entities, nuclear research organizations, and academic institutions remain secondary but persistent targets.
  The May 2025 India-Pakistan military conflict (Operation Sindoor) has materially raised the geopolitical stakes driving Patchwork's operational mandate. In the 88-hour war's aftermath, India's strategic intelligence requirements regarding Pakistan military capabilities, 
  nuclear posture, and diplomatic alignments have intensified.

  The introduction of Brute Ratel C4 in 2024 marks the most significant arsenal escalation in Patchwork's history, transitioning the group from a "copy-paste APT" toward a more professional offensive capability profile. 
  This escalation, combined with post-Operation Sindoor geopolitical urgency, suggests Patchwork's operational tempo and sophistication will increase through 2026–2027.
</p>
