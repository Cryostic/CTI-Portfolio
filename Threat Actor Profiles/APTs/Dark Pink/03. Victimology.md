<h2> Victim Profile Characteristics</h2>
<p>
  Across documented attacks, Dark Pink shared several of these consistent characteristics:
  <ul>
    <li> Organizations holding classified or sensitive government/military communications </li>
    <li> Entities with diplomatic relationships spanning ASEAN and European Partners </li>
    <li> Organizations with likely poor Cybersecurity Maturity relative to the sophistication of the attack </li>
    <li> Targets selected for intelligence values, not financial or destructive purposes </li>
  </ul>
</p>
