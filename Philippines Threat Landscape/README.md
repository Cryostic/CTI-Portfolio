# Philippines Cyber Threat Intelligence Landscape

An open-source intelligence repository dedicated to the continuous monitoring, tactical deconstruction, and strategic mapping of cyber threat campaigns actively targeting Philippine enterprise networks, critical infrastructure, and public sector assets.

## Project Objectives

* **Monitor Regional Cybercrime:** Track local financial fraud, initial access broker (IAB) monetization pipelines, and active ransomware networks.
* **Track State-Sponsored Threat Actors:** Monitor Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs) operating inside the ASEAN / APAC / Philippines theater.
* **Analyze Technical Campaigns:** Dissect real-world intrusions, malware execution workflows, and multi-stage distribution chains.
* **Identify Vulnerable Victim Sectors:** Map historical targeting trends across government, banking, maritime logistics, and defense.
* **Produce Strategic Intelligence:** Deliver actionable analytical assessments correlating kinetic geopolitics with digital battlefields.

---

## Core Priority Intelligence Requirements (PIRs)

### PIR 1: Active Threat Attribution
* **Requirement:** Which state-sponsored clusters and cybercrime syndicates are actively demonstrating operational intent against Philippine entities?

### PIR 2: Structural Victimology & Target Profiles
* **Requirement:** Which sectors are bearing the brunt of ongoing campaigns, and what corporate asset types are primarily sought?

### PIR 3: Tactical TTP Trends & Evasion Mechanics
* **Requirement:** What specific tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) such as memory-only fileless implants or Adversary-in-the-Middle (phishing kits are threat actors using to bypass regional perimeter defenses?
